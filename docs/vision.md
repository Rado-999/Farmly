# Farmly Vision

Farmly exists to reconnect people with real food and real farmers.

Modern food systems often feel industrial, disconnected, and difficult to trust. People rarely know:

- where their food comes from
- how it was grown
- who produced it
- what was used during production

Farmly is built around the belief that trust can be rebuilt through transparency, storytelling, and community.

## Product direction

This is not intended to feel like a traditional marketplace.

Farmly is designed as a trust-first platform where local farmers can:

- share their farming journey
- document how products are grown
- upload videos and updates
- educate consumers
- build long-term trust

Consumers should feel emotionally connected to the people behind their food.

The experience should feel closer to:

- visiting family in the countryside
- eating food directly from the garden
- trusting the source completely

## Farmer profiles

Every farmer profile should feel personal and authentic.

Each farmer can eventually have:

- a public profile
- a product catalog
- a video library
- farming updates
- ratings and reviews

## Product philosophy

Products are not just listings.

Every product should tell a story. A single product can include:

- associated farming videos
- a growing timeline
- production methods
- fertilizer information
- harvest updates
- seasonal availability

The goal is for consumers to feel connected to the full lifecycle of what they buy.

## Marketplace philosophy

Farmly should never feel like:

- a generic ecommerce site
- a mass industrial food marketplace
- a growth-hacked catalog optimized only for sales

It should prioritize:

- quality
- trust
- transparency
- authenticity
- community

## Initial audience

The first audience is everyday people in Bulgaria who want:

- better food
- trusted sources
- authentic local products
- a real connection with farmers

## Monetization

Initial monetization:

- farmer subscriptions

Potential future monetization:

- promoted farmer profiles
- verification systems
- platform transactions
- logistics support

## Future direction

Potential long-term features:

- farmer verification badges
- podcasts and interviews
- educational blog content
- seasonal food content
- delivery and payments
- mobile applications
- farmer communities

## Technical direction

The product should:

- be mobile-first
- feel modern and fast
- prioritize video content
- support scalable growth
- use reusable components
- maintain clean architecture

## Design direction

The interface should feel:

- organic
- modern
- calm
- minimal
- trustworthy

Avoid:

- flashy ecommerce design
- aggressive marketing UI
- cluttered layouts

Use:

- warm spacing
- rounded corners
- soft shadows
- natural imagery
- clean typography

## Core principle

Trust is more important than scale.  
Quality is more important than quantity.  
Authenticity is more important than growth hacks.
