# Vision

This platform exists to reconnect people with real food and real farmers.

Modern food systems feel industrial, disconnected, and untrustworthy.
People no longer know:
- where their food comes from
- how it was grown
- who produced it
- what was used during production

Our mission is to rebuild trust between farmers and consumers through transparency, storytelling, and community.

This is not a traditional marketplace.

The platform is designed as a trust-first social platform where local farmers can:
- share their farming journey
- document how products are grown
- upload videos and updates
- educate consumers
- build long-term trust

Consumers should feel emotionally connected to the people behind their food.

The experience should feel similar to:
- visiting your grandmother in the countryside
- eating food directly from the garden
- trusting the source completely

The platform should create feelings of:
- authenticity
- warmth
- nostalgia
- trust
- calmness
- quality

# Product Direction

The platform is social-first and marketplace-second.

Users should not only visit to buy products.
They should visit to:
- discover farmers
- follow farming journeys
- watch seasonal updates
- learn about food production
- engage with authentic content

Commerce should happen naturally as trust grows.

# Farmer Profiles

Every farmer has:
- a public profile
- a product catalog
- a video library
- farming updates
- ratings and reviews

Farmer profiles should feel personal and authentic.

# Product Philosophy

Products are not just listings.

Every product should tell a story.

Products may include:
- associated farming videos
- growing timeline
- production methods
- fertilizer information
- harvest updates
- seasonal availability

Example:
A tomato product may include videos showing:
- planting
- growth stages
- watering
- harvesting

Consumers should feel connected to the full lifecycle of the product.

# Marketplace Philosophy

This platform should never feel like:
- OLX
- generic ecommerce
- mass industrial food selling

Avoid:
- fake bio marketing
- aggressive sales tactics
- corporate marketplace feeling

Prioritize:
- quality
- trust
- transparency
- authenticity
- community

# Initial Audience

The first target audience is everyday people in Bulgaria who want:
- better food
- trusted sources
- authentic local products
- connection with farmers

# Monetization

Initial monetization:
- farmer subscriptions

Future monetization may include:
- promoted farmer profiles
- verification systems
- platform transactions
- logistics support

# Future Features

Potential future features:
- farmer verification badges
- podcasts and interviews
- educational blog content
- seasonal food content
- delivery and payments
- mobile applications
- farmer communities

# Technical Direction

The platform should:
- be mobile-first
- feel modern and fast
- prioritize video content
- support scalable growth
- use reusable components
- maintain clean architecture

# Design Direction

The UI should feel:
- organic
- modern
- calm
- minimal
- trustworthy

Avoid:
- flashy ecommerce design
- aggressive marketing UI
- cluttered layouts

Use:
- warm spacing
- rounded corners
- soft shadows
- natural imagery
- clean typography

# Core Principle

Trust is more important than scale.
Quality is more important than quantity.
Authenticity is more important than growth hacks.
